  1. Home
  2. Umngot river

Description

Umngot River is a prime fishing spot for both Indian and Bangladeshi fishermen. The river not only flows through green ambience but also shines like silver. Also, boating in the crystal clear water is an experience of lifetime. The ride starts from the border point and went up the river till a small island type place where there were boulders all over the place. While boating, one can clearly see the marine life in the water. The town attracts large number of tourists especially in the month of March-April as it hosts the annual boat race on the Umngot River.
